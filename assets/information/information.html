About Moi

I was born in India and moved to Canada when I was 12 years old. My love for physics began in high school after reading books by Stephan Hawkins, Brian Greene, and Leonard Suskind. The Theoretical Minimum books are one of my favorite set of books which I highly recommend for anyone who wants to learn physics. Also, the Brief History of Time is good book to get a brief understanding of the universe.

I'm also a big fan of Sherlock Holmes which is why I started learning violin. I also love to paint when I have free time.
